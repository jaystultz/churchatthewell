For centuries, wells have served as a source of life. All around the world, people walked for hours in extreme conditions, simply to make a draw of its water. The water that flows is essential for all living things, and is the fabric of our physical being. Wells served as a place of hope, healing, and restoration. They can change your life and your community.

In John 4, Jacob’s well was a source of life to the Samaritan woman, as she draws water to meet her physical needs. But the living water she received from Jesus’s well gave her eternal life. This gift of eternal life is available to all.

"but whoever drinks the water I give them will never thirst. Indeed, the water I give them will become in them a spring of water welling up to eternal life.”

-Jesus of Nazareth, John 4:14

Church at the Well welcomes everyone regardless of your background, circumstance, or status. We are a non-denominational, scripturally based assembly with a heart to love others and to serve the Father.
